Teachings of Ramana Maharshi

Who Am I?

“The mind will subside only by means of the inquiry ‘Who am I?’. The thought ‘Who am I?’ will destroy all other thoughts, and like the stick used for stirring the burning pyre, it will itself in the end get destroyed. Then, there will arise Self-realization.”

Be Still

“Be still and know that you are God. The Self is the Heart. It is self-luminous. Be still, and the truth will reveal itself.”

The Nature of the Self

“The Self itself is God. All that is required of you is to give up the thought that you are this body. When that false identification is removed, only the Self remains — infinite and eternal.”
